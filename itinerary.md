ITALY — TWO WEEKS: WINE COUNTRY + TWO COASTS
Sat 3 Oct – Sun 18 Oct 2026 · 15 nights
Route: Florence → Val d'Orcia (Montalcino) → Argentario (Maremma) → Cinque Terre → Santa Margherita/Portofino (Liguria) → Milan
Fly: Icelandair KEF 09:00 → MXP 15:10 (out) · Wizz Air MXP 16:00 → KEF 18:20 (back)

WHAT CHANGED IN THIS VERSION
 - Umbria dropped. All wine-country time is now Tuscany — more chic-hotel choice, more iconic scenery.
 - No remote-winery drives. Wine happens at your hotel's own cellar and at in-town / walkable cellars only.
 - Day 1 ends in Florence (direct ~2 h train), not a 5 h haul down to Perugia.
 - Two coasts, ordered to finish near Milan: Maremma beach in the middle, chic Riviera at the end.
   Final drive to the airport is ~2 h 40 instead of ~3 h 45.

WHERE YOU SLEEP
 Florence .............. 1 night (arrival only)
 Val d'Orcia ........... HERO: private vineyard villa/farmhouse w/ pool via Airbnb (6 nights) — book early
 Argentario ............ modest coast hotel: Argentario Golf Resort & Spa (year-round) — La Roqqa if splurging (4 nights)
 Cinque Terre .......... Monterosso al Mare — only village with a beach + real hotels (2 nights, car-free)
 Santa Margherita ...... harbour hotel; Portofino by boat (2 nights)

NOTES
 - Car: pick up Firenze SMN (day 2), one-way drop LA SPEZIA (day 12) — all-domestic Italian one-way, so
   the fee is small (~€11–50, not the €200+ cross-border kind). Book via a consolidator (AutoEurope etc.)
   that shows the one-way fee before you pay. Confirm La Spezia office hours (Wed 14 Oct afternoon drop).
   FLORENCE ZTL: depots sit at the restricted-zone edge — follow the signed exit route out, do NOT cut
   through the centre or you'll get camera fines by post. Don't drive into Cinque Terre villages / Portofino.
 - Trains: fares open ~90–120 days out, so Day 1 (Oct 3) + Day 16 (Oct 18) are BOOKABLE NOW. Grab cheap
   "Super Economy" fares early (€15–20 vs €70–80 last-minute). Day 16: Sta Margherita → Milano C.le is
   direct ~2 h 04 (~17/day); take ~09:00 → C.le ~11:05 → Malpensa Express ~11:25 → MXP ~12:15 (buffer to 13:30).
 - Cinque Terre is car-free by design: villages linked by the frequent Cinque Terre Express train, boats
   (Apr–Oct), and footpaths. Get the Cinque Terre Card. Check trail closures before you count on a hike.
 - Lodging strategy: splurge on the HERO (Val d'Orcia villa), stay modest on the coast.
   Book the villa first — good vineyard homes with a pool for early Oct go fast. Vet reviews +
   Superhost status, and check the drive to Montalcino (some are 20+ min down a gravel strada bianca).
   Optional 2nd splurge if you want one: Porto Roca, Monterosso (cliff infinity pool, only 2 nights).
 - Wine, no driving: town enoteche in Montalcino and the walkable Vino Nobile cellars under
   Montepulciano; book one estate tasting for a driver-light day. No remote estate marathons.
 - Seasonal: some Maremma/Riviera hotels close late October — La Roqqa runs May–Oct (your dates OK);
   Argentario Golf Resort and the Santa Margherita town hotels run year-round. Confirm when booking.
 - Baggage: Wizz Air is strict — ship a case of wine home or pre-book a checked bag.
